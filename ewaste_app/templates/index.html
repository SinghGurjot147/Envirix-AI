Enter the specs, get a valuation

The model weighs hardware specs the same way a refurbisher would — then age and condition adjust that figure down to today's resale value.

Why this matters

Specs set the baseline, age and condition set the discount

The valuation model predicts what this device is worth in its original spec configuration. Age and condition are then applied as a depreciation adjustment to estimate what it's worth right now.
